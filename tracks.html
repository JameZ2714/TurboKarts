What Tracks do we have?

Circut de Monaco

This is one of our circuts. We are proud to host events here because we took insparation from the Monaco grand prix. This is a special track where many races internationally love and enjoy the fights on the track. The track has 12 right turns and 7 left turns which make up the 19 turns that the circut is composed of. Monacos Circut is famous in F1 and all of the famous drivers who have manuvered the circut.


Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps

Our second circut is that of Belgiums F1 circut. This is a circut in which many different races take place. It is well known for the Radillon Eau Rouge which is composed of the third fourth and fifth turn on the circut. The circut has 20 turns, 11 right turns and 9 left turns, all of these corners are mainly high speed corners usually only requiering a slight lift off the gas. This aspect of the track is what makes it so spacial and intresting.
